Introducing BizConnect

Create a business card in a few simple steps.
Make it online, keep it online, share it online.

Next

Hold all your Business Cards on your Phone.

Featuring an intuitive NFC transfer method.

In this age of technology, it just doesn't make sense to be exchanging contact information using paper that costs not only money, but our valuable space. Wouldn't it make sense to keep all of your business contacts on your phone? That's why we're proud to introduce BizConnect.

When information on our business cards changes, we are forced to buy an entirely new set. On top of this, people will be left with outdated cards. This is just one of the things that is exciting about BizConnect: update your business cards on the fly and push changes remotely.

Where is this awesome app available?

Find us on the Google Play Store (link pending)

Currently, we support Android OS, the world's most used operating system at 90% market share. Using an iPhone? Don't worry! Our developers are hard at work to add QR functionality to both the Android app as well as an upcoming iOS app.
